The Story Behind

This is my first ever project, built using Python, Flask, HTML, and some Tailwind CSS. It's a solution to a personal challenge I faced - the frustration of feeling like my whole day was wasted just because I woke up an hour late than planned.

What started as a simple idea turned into an intense 15-hour coding marathon (from 10 PM to 2 PM the next day!). While it may seem simple, every line of code represents my learning journey and determination to solve a real problem.

Future Plans

While this version is basic, I have plans to expand it into a fully AI-powered scheduling assistant with advanced features and functionalities. Your feedback and support will help shape its future!
